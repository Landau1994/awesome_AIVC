# Drug Discovery Applications

## Definition
> Drug discovery applications of AIVC involve using virtual cell models to predict drug responses, identify therapeutic targets, optimize compounds, and accelerate the drug development pipeline through in-silico experimentation.

## Key Ideas
- **Target identification**: Find genes/proteins to modulate
- **Response prediction**: Forecast cellular response to drugs
- **Combination therapy**: Predict drug combinations
- **Personalized medicine**: Patient-specific predictions
